About Us

We are a non-profit organization made up of adults, high school students, and college students devoted to helping various people in need. We focus on helping refugees acclimate to their new homes, whether that be helping them learn new languages, study certain subjects, or run donation drives. If you have any other questions, feel free to email us at student.center.mail@gmail.com
