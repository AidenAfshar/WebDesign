How it works for students

Students will either have lessons Online or In-Person (San Diego)

Online Lessons

In-Person Lessons

Once you have signed up (in the link under the students button), you will be contacted by a tutor to schedule a lesson. These lessons can take place over phone calls, whatsapp, or zoom. They may be group lessons or 1 on 1 lessons dependong on your choice when you signed up, and will usually last anywhere from 30 minutes to 1 hour. During the lesson, you will have a worksheet, passage, which your tutor will help you work through. Additionally, if you are a student and need homework help, we would be more than happy to help in any way we can.

Once you have signed up (in the link under the "students" button), you will be notified by an organizer about upcoming events. In these events, we usually bring a few tutors to run group or individual lessons in community centers or parks. Since many of the refugees we currently help are located in hotels, we often hold the lessons there, and will also bring a few toys to keep young children busy while others learn.
